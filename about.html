About Youth In Action Lesotho

A Movement Born from Belief

Our Story

Youth In Action Lesotho is a youth-led movement that empowers young Basotho by sharing real, relatable stories of resilience, growth, and success. We believe every young person deserves to see themselves in stories of triumph and know that their dreams are valid and achievable.

Founded with a passion for transforming lives through authentic storytelling, we've made it our mission to reach students in schools across Lesotho, providing them with inspiration and mentorship that shows them what's possible.

What We Believe

Authenticity

Real stories from real people create real change. We share unfiltered narratives of struggle and success.

Empowerment

Every young person has potential. Our role is to help them recognize, believe in, and unleash it.

Community

We believe in the power of collective action. Together, we can shape a brighter future for Lesotho.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of responsibility and respect in all our interactions with youth and institutions.

Growth

We believe in continuous learning and development for ourselves and the youth we serve.

Resilience

Challenges are opportunities. We celebrate the journey of overcoming adversity.

Our Approach

School Visits & Community Engagement

We visit schools across Lesotho, bringing inspiring speakers and facilitating interactive sessions where students can ask questions and share their own stories. Our approach is interactive, engaging, and designed to spark meaningful conversations about potential and possibility.

Storytelling Methodology

Our storytelling methodology focuses on real, relatable narratives. We train facilitators to share experiences that resonate with young audiences—stories of academic achievement, personal growth, overcoming challenges, and celebrating success.

Youth-Centered Programs

Every program is designed with youth in mind. We listen to what young people need, create spaces for them to be heard, and provide platforms for them to share their own stories and connect with mentors who believe in their potential.
