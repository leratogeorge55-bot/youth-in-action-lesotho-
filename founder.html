Founder Story

Lerato G. Mohlomi is a purpose-driven youth leader committed to empowering young people by highlighting resilience, growth, and triumph over adversity. With a deep passion for youth development and personal transformation, Lerato has dedicated his life to inspiring the next generation of Basotho leaders.

Before founding Youth In Action Lesotho, Lerato led the Psycho-Social Support Youth Programme, an initiative rooted in youth development and well-being. Through this program, he worked directly with young people, addressing their emotional needs, building confidence, and creating safe spaces for growth and healing.

His journey as a youth leader is itself a story of resilience and achievement. Lerato's own experiences of overcoming challenges and pursuing excellence inspired him to create a movement that would share similar stories with thousands of Basotho youth—stories that would show them that their dreams are not just valid, but achievable.

Today, through Youth In Action Lesotho, Lerato continues his mission to ignite belief in young people and create pathways to success for all who dare to dream.
