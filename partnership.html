Programme Purpose

To inspire students through real stories of growth, discipline, and success, showing every young person that their dreams are valid and their potential is real. All engagement will be responsible, honest, and respectful.

Student Protection

Student participation is voluntary. No student will be pressured, exposed to harm, ridicule, or exploitation. We are committed to creating a safe, supportive environment for all young people we work with.

Media & Consent

No student will be recorded or published without approval from the institution and where required, parental consent. We respect privacy and handle all media responsibly.

Online Safety

Youth In Action Lesotho will protect participants from cyberbullying and remove harmful content. We maintain high standards of digital safety and responsibility.
